You’ve never experienced Las Vegas like this. Get ready for a trivia event that mixes the best of both trivia worlds with puzzling and the power of observation. From the Peloponnesian War to Pizza the Hut, we have you covered. Join a medal-winning quest that combines a wide set of trivia knowledge with a sense of fun and adventure.

Welcome to the “Incredible Not-a-Race”, where time is limited but speed is not required. This two-hour circuit will showcase vegas from new point of view with the unique charm that only vegas can offer. Teams of up to 4 will need to work together to crack codes while maximizing player strengths in tests of trivia and teamwork.

Sign up today for the incredible not a race and get ready to walk, not run...

The NOT-A-RACE is an activity where groups travel within a defined area of the southern portion of the Las Vegas Strip for the purpose of competition in trivia challenges, and to accomplish other tasks and observations. NOT-A-RACE will take place beginning after 10pm Friday, August 4th until the wee hours on Saturday, August 5th, 2017 beginning at a location near the Trivia Championships of North America “TCONA” activities at the Tropicana Hotel, Resort and Casino. Teams will start the course at specific assigned times and have sufficient time to navigate among the challenges by walking or provided transit prior to 1am. There are no specific time limits between activities other than the entire duration of the contest and no difference in scoring based upon faster or slower completion. Participants are encouraged to walk at a normal pace and not to run, jump, climb, or otherwise exert themselves, and to limit their time outdoors.

TEAMS ARE LIMITED TO MAXIMUM FOUR MEMBERS. TEAMS OF FOUR WILL TAKE PRIORITY OVER TEAMS OF SMALLER NUMBERS. IF THERE ARE MORE TEAMS THAT SIGN UP THAN SLOTS AVAILABLE, TEAMS WILL BE SELECTED BY LOTTERY. The team will designate a captain and a second in command, one of which must be present at any given checkpoint. Teams may have members join their group in progress should they be involved in other activities at TCONA, and likewise have team members who depart for other activities as needed.
